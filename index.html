The Cognitive Assistive Robotic Environments (CARE) Group focus is on enabling robotic, autonomous and interactive systems to provide user-centered living support. We lead Open Ambient Assisted Living (OpenAAL), an open lab to accellerate the creation of innovative, sustainable and affordable solutions for healthy ageing and indipendent living systems from the UK National Robotarium.
